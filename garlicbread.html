Garlic Bread

A long, detailed description isn't neccessarily needed for this recipe. Most people on this Earth know what garlic bread is and I'd venture to say that most people love it. Here is a basic recipes for garlic bread that I like to use when preparing a meal that will pair well with this yeasty, garlc treat

Ingredients

  • Italian or French bread
  • Salted butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Minced garlic
  • Fresh parsley

How to prepare

  1. Slice your Italian or French bread in half
  2. Mix your butter, garlic and parsley and parmesan cheese together in a bowl and spread the mixture over the two halves of bread
  3. Heat in the oven for 10 minutes on 350F
